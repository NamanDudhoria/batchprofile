About The Institute

Established in 1963 by the Govt of India, IIFT is an autonomous institution under the Ministry of Commerce & Industry dedicated to enhancing the country's foreign trade management and global business skill.

  • Accredited with A+ grade by NAAC
  • Category-1 institution by UGC/MHRD
  • MA Economics program launched in 2018
